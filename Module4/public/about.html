What Are Sandbox Games?

Definition

Sandbox games are video games that give players a high degree of creativity and freedom to explore and interact with the world around them.

Game Types

  • Creative building games (e.g., Minecraft)
  • Simulation games (e.g., The Sims)
  • Open-world adventure games (e.g., Skyrim)

Key Features

Players can craft, build, explore, and experiment without fixed objectives. The main goal is freedom and creativity.
